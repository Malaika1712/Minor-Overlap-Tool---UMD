Minor Overlap Checker
How to use this tool:
  • Red = Satisfies CS major requirement
  • Green, Purple, Blue, etc. = Satisfies minor requirement (color matches minor)
  • No courses can overlap between selected minors.
  • This tool helps you check overlap between your CS track and selected minors.
  • Final consultation requires advisor approval.
  • ULC requirements for the CS major do NOT count towards overlapping credit counts.
